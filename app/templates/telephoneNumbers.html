Now that you know how to select numbers, you can combine multiple search terms into one to select more complicated expressions. For example '\d-\d' selects a number, then the character "-", then another number, so it would match 8-4, or 9-2. Try selecting all the telephone numbers on the page. Can I have yo Numbah?
